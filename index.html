Our Technology

Direct Air Capture (DAC)

We use advanced Direct Air Capture technology to extract CO₂ directly from the atmosphere. This carbon is the building block for our synthetic fuel, ensuring a closed-loop, carbon-neutral process.

Fischer-Tropsch + Hydrocracking

Our process combines CO₂ with renewable hydrogen using the Fischer-Tropsch synthesis, followed by hydrocracking to produce high-quality, carbon-neutral gasoline compatible with existing infrastructure.

Vision & Impact

Environmental Benefits

Our fuel reduces lifecycle CO₂ emissions by up to 90% compared to fossil gasoline, helping combat climate change without requiring new infrastructure.

Economic Opportunities

By leveraging existing fuel distribution systems, we create jobs and drive economic growth in the clean energy sector.

Scalable Solution

Our technology is designed to scale globally, providing a sustainable alternative to fossil fuels for transportation and industry.
